# Example queries

Use these after ingesting `sample_data/pdfs/sample_circuits.pdf` (or your own PDFs).

1. **What is the difference between series and parallel resistors?**
2. **Summarize the main concepts on page 1.**
3. **Is there a diagram? What does it show?** (exercises image extraction + captions)
4. **What does page 2 say about capacitors?**
5. **List key terms related to electric circuits from the document.**

For technical PDFs with figures, ask explicitly about **labels, arrows, and relationships** in diagrams to leverage multimodal captions.
